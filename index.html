    About Me

    Farida Ibrahim is a Cairo, Egypt-based artist. Born on October 28, 1996, and currently living in Egypt, studied fine arts at Helwan University painting department. She participated in 15 group exhibitions in different cities around Egypt. She also participated in recycling projects for sustainable art and human development initiatives and volunteered as an art trainer for orphan children organized by the Ministry of Youth and Sports.
